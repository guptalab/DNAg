Born: December 31, 1945 (age 67), California
Children: Lindsey Adleman, Jennifer Adleman, Stephanie Adleman
Education: University of California, Berkeley (1976), University of California, Berkeley (1968)
Awards: Turing Award

Eric Winfree

An American computer scientist, bioengineer, and professor at California Institute of Technology. He is a leading researcher into DNA computing and DNA nanotechnology
Born: September 26, 1969 (age 43)
Parents: Arthur Winfree
Education: University of Chicago
Awards: MacArthur Fellowship

Winfree presents an experimental investigation of algorithmic self-assembly. A competition experiment suggests that an individual logical step can proceed correctly by self-assembly, while a companion experiment demonstrates that unpatterned two dimensional lattices of DNA will self-assemble and can be visualized







Anne Condon

Computer Scientist
Anne Elizabeth Condon is an Irish-Canadian - Computer Scientist, professor, and head of the UBC Computer Science Department. Her research focuses on computational complexity theory and bioinformatics. Education: University of Washington, University College Cork - National University of Ireland, Cork

Also her Research work in the paper on Stochastic Local Search Algorithms for DNA Word Design, with Dan C. Tulpan, Holger H. Hoos [1] were the building blocks for us in the modification of the SLS for building a codebook which improvised to form one suitable for our use.
